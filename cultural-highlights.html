Barcelona's Cultural Highlights

Seafood Paella

An authentic traditional catalonian dish. This dish is a staple in Barcrelona. It's a colorful rice dish bursting with clams, mussles, shrimp, and smoky chorizo (saffron for flavor as well). It's a must try and no one makes it better than Barcelona.

15 Minute City

Barcelona's city is one of the best 15 minute cities in the world with it's grid like design. The city has been designed for walkability which means everything you might need on a regular basis is always within a 15 minute walk.

Antoni Gaudí

Antoni Gaudí is a renowned Catalan architect with a very distinctive style characterized by freedom of form, strange colors/textures, and organic unity (flow). He is responsible for much of the beautiful funky buildings one can find in Barcelona and was the one who designed the Sagrada Família.

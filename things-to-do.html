So. Much. To. Do.

Must See Architecture

Park Güell

A complex of parks, gardens and funky architecture. It is a harmonous park full of complex shapes, tiles, and sculptures.

Sagrada Família

The largest unfinished catholic church in the world. It is a one-of-a-kind temple with jaw dropping architecture.

Football

Spotify Camp Nou

Famous stadium for La Liga club FC Barcelona. Get tickets and attend a football game with an atmosphere like no other.

Beaches!

Playa de la Barceloneta

Golden sand beach full of fun activities all over. There are tons of shops, restaurants and bike stations near by as well.

Bogatell Beach

A less crowded beach than others in Barcelona that is comftorable and easy to relax on.
